The Friends of Strong Public Library are the many people who donate to the library in many different ways.  Some give of their time, some give needed materials and items, and still others give money.
The continued success of the Strong Public Library is dependent on these many donations.  If you are not currently a Friend of the Library and wish to join, please contact the library and let us know how you would like to help.
